# Helwan Prism Ultimate 🐧✨

Helwan Prism Ultimate is a mind-bending physics puzzle game developed exclusively for Helwan Linux. Take control of high-tech lasers, rotate mirrors, and navigate through obstacles to hit the target. It’s where logic meets light!

## 🎮 How to Play

The goal is simple: Hit the red target with your laser beam.

- **Move Mirrors:** Use your mouse to position mirrors and reflect the beam.
- **Rotate:** Fine-tune the angles to create the perfect path.
- **Win:** Once the laser hits the target, it will turn bright green, and you'll clear the level!
- **Watch the Clock:** Solve the puzzle before the timer runs out.

## 🚀 Features

- **Smart Physics:** Realistic laser reflection and collision logic.
- **Saeed's Edition:** Featuring the "Genius Penguin" mascot playing the game right in your menu.
- **Lightweight:** Optimized for the Cinnamon, GNOME, and Xfce desktops of Helwan Linux.
- **Demo Mode:** Watch the computer solve complex puzzles on its own.
